About Us

ArtHouseLabs is a client-oriented Art outsource studio that specializes in full cycle Game Art Production starting from 2D Concepts and Illustrations to animated 3D characters and Environment.

We guarantee the most powerful management at all stages of production, obtaining the optimal result in the given terms and budgets. We use simple and transparent flow, constant quality control at all stages of production, and selection of the ideal performer for the task. We provide high motivation and constant growth for employees and the most effective pipeline for partners.
